Biography

I have recently completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science/Software Engineering at Zhejiang University. I obtained an excellent score of 640/700 (91.42%) in my PhD coursework. I was honored with the International Student Honorary Title of Academic Excellence at ZJU for the 2022/2023 academic year. I was also honored with the International Student Honorary Title of Research Excellence at ZJU for the 2024/2025 academic year. I completed my master's degree in Software Engineering at Northeastern University with a CGPA of 3.75/4.00 (93.75%). I received the NEU Outstanding International Student Award during my Master's studies.

I secured second position in the International Track of the 7th Huawei Cup China AI Innovation Competition. I have authored nine publications, eight of which I am the first author, including works in Q1 journals such as IEEE Transactions on Multimedia, Neurocomputing, Multimedia Systems, and Computer-Aided Design. My research interests lie in artificial intelligence, computer vision, emotion recognition, and generative models. I have worked on multimodal learning, human motion generation, and intelligent recommendation systems, with applications spanning embodied intelligence and human-centered AI.

I am seeking academic and industrial opportunities in artificial intelligence, with a focus on multimodal systems, generative AI, and intelligent interactive technologies. You can view my CV here: Download CV. Please feel free to contact me if you believe my background would be a good fit for your team.
